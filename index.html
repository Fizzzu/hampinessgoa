About Us!


We, Anziya and Uzma introduce you to a brand that will cater to all your gifting needs!
Our gift hampers are sure to provide the happiest of moments to you and your loved ones.
Chocolate qualifies as the perfect gifting solution for any occasion – be it a festival, a wedding, a baby shower or simply celebrating life.


We, at Hampiness-ChocoFab present to you fresh, finely handcrafted, premium quality homemade chocolates and much more packed attractively to suit your occasion.
Customized gifting ideas are one of the most trending things that you can enjoy with us!
We offer an array of luxury handmade chocolates with exuberant taste, quality, art and texture.
Customer satisfaction is our driving force.
